Our Grant Programs

Brooklyn Arts Council grants strive to support local artists and small arts organizations whose work enhances the cultural climate of our borough, city, state, and nation. These awards encourage professional development, community growth, and fellowship among artists. Learn more about our Community Arts Grants, Creative Equations Fund, and new Brooklyn Empower Fund here.

Learn More

Join Us!

Brooklyn Arts Council empowers artists and creative communities. We promote equitable access to the arts through grant-making, educational programs, performances, and professional development for artists. When you support Brooklyn Arts Council, you help nurture the careers of independent artists who are working within their communities to bring accessible cultural programming to every corner of Brooklyn. Please join us in our efforts to ensure that art and artists endure and thrive in our shared community. Brooklyn Arts Council is grateful for your support and appreciates your generosity.
